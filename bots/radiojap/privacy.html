When you use RadioJAP, we automatically collect certain data to ensure the bot functions properly:

The data we collect is used solely for the purpose of providing and improving the RadioJAP service. Specifically, we use your data to:

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or otherwise disclose your personal information to third parties, except as necessary to:

We take the security of your data seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.
